Appearance

Rem has medium length sky blue hair that covers her right eye, large light blue eyes, and young features.

Personality

Rem tends to speak in a superficially polite manner and sometimes acts without thinking.

History

Rem lived along with her father, mother, and her sister Ram in an Oni village.

Abilities

Rem is skilled at Water Magic and Roswaal estimates her skill as being in the lower high.

Trivia

Rem's birthday is February 2, the same as her twin sister Ram.
